Our Mission

The mission of the Oconee River Land Trust is to conserve natural lands to protect water quality, preserve wildlife habitats, and enhance the quality of our lives and those of future generations.

With the Tallassee Park Mobile App, we hope to carry on this mission by creating a way to engage the Athens-Clarke and Oconee County communities with this new park. By educating the public about the importance of environmental convervation, we can come together as a community to preserve not only the wealth of vital habitats contained within this park, but the environment around us in our everyday lives.

Background

Our team is developing a cross-platform mobile app for the Oconee River Land Trust for their newly created park in Athens: Tallassee Park. The land is currently a conservation easement, meaning that the land trust is in charge of protecting the land from any development that may cause harm to the natural environment within the property. Our goal with this app is to engage the Athens community with this new park and educate them on the importance of conservation easements. To create our cross-platform app, we are leveraging the React Native library to develop for Android and iOS simultaneously.

The App

Designed for iOS and Android

We developed the Tallassee Park Mobile App using   React Native - a branch of the React framework developed by Facebook to support seamless cross-platform development. You'll find every feature present in both versions of the App.

Features

  Map of the property featuring Sattelite, Feature, and Topographical layers

  Park info including hours & parking information

  Activities for children of all ages with supplies lists and instructions

  Explore the park with information about Flora, Fauna, and natural processes taking place in the park

  Track your progress as you engage with the park and learn

  Offline data caching for areas of low cellular data connection

This color palette forms the basis of our brand identity. We chose these closers to create a simple and earthy feel for our print materials, presentations, and applications. The palette encompasses the main colors people encounter in nature, thus giving users a feel of Tallassee Park at the touch of their fingers. We made sure to have a nice contrast in colors, with deeper tones such as Black Magic, Kombu Green, and Baja Blue, alongside our neutral colors Volkswagen Kalahari Beige, Tea Leaves, and Quarter Powder Blue.
